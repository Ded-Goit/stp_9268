Candy Kingdoms

Explore a World Made of Sugar & Dreams

Each world brings a new theme and a different kind of challenge:

Licorice Castle – guarded by stubborn blockers and tricky puzzles

Marshmallow Bay – water-themed sweets and floating goals

Unlock new lands with each milestone and watch your kingdom grow!
